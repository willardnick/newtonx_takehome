Never-Submitted Rate by Channel
Unknown (59.8%) and Paid Social (52.3%) have the worst rates. Referral (24.7%) is best — referral experts may have clearer expectations set by the referring expert.
Why This Is the #1 Priority

It's entirely within expert control. Unlike Verified → Activated (which depends on demand matching), an expert can submit their profile right now.

Session data proves it's a now-or-never moment. 0% session break rate means experts who don't submit immediately will never come back to do it. This isn't procrastination — it's abandonment.

Potential bug? Is there a UX issue preventing submission? The step is fast (median 13h) for those who complete it, suggesting it's trivial when it works.

Re-engagement opportunity: 2,321 experts with completed profiles who can be re-contacted. Even converting 25% would add 580 experts to the verification pipeline — a 15.5% lift to verification volume.
